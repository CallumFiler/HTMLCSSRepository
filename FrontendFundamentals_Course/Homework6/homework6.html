Tacos should look vibrant and inviting, with warm, slightly crisp tortillas encasing a colorful mix of ingredients like juicy, seasoned meat or fresh veggies, topped with vibrant garnishes such as cilantro, onions, and a squeeze of lime. They should smell mouthwateringly fragrant, a blend of savory spices, grilled meat, and fresh herbs.
