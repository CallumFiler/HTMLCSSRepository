Introduction

In "The Fellowship of the Ring," the first book of The Lord of the Rings, a young hobbit named Frodo Baggins inherits the One Ring, a powerful and evil artifact sought by the Dark Lord Sauron. To prevent Sauron from reclaiming it and conquering Middle-earth, Frodo must leave his home and embark on a dangerous journey to destroy the Ring.

Boromir

Unfortunately, Boromir gets deleted from the story. He is a valiant warrior from Gondor who tries to take the Ring from Frodo, but redeems himself by sacrificing his life to save Merry and Pippin from the orcs.
